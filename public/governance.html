HJS Responsibility Protocol · v1.0 Beta

Governance Model

The HJS Responsibility Protocol is open and foundation-governed.

Principles

The protocol is open. The specification is publicly available.

Multiple independent implementations are permitted.

The foundation acts as steward, not owner.

The protocol cannot be privately enclosed.

Foundation

HJS Foundation (Singapore CLG)

UEN: 202600000C

The foundation holds the protocol assets and ensures its neutrality. It does not operate the commercial service.
