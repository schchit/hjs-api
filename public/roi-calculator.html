How it works

How is the tier calculated?

HJS uses tiered pricing: Experience (0-100 anchors, $0.03), Standard (101-1K, $0.02), Bulk (1K-10K, $0.01), Enterprise (10K+, $0.005). The more you anchor, the less you pay per anchor.

What counts as a compliance incident?

Regulatory investigations, customer disputes, audit findings, or legal actions where you need to prove your AI made correct decisions. Average costs include legal fees ($20-50K), audit costs ($10-30K), and operational disruption.

Can I reduce network fees?

Yes. Batch anchoring with Merkle trees reduces network fees by up to 99%. Contact us for enterprise batch processing options.
